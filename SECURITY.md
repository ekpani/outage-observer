# Security Policy

We take the security of Outage Observer seriously and welcome good-faith reports
of potential vulnerabilities.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email **hi@ekpani.com**, or open a private report via this repository's
**Security** tab ("Report a vulnerability"). Please include steps to reproduce
and any relevant logs or proof-of-concept.

## What to expect

- We aim to acknowledge reports within **3 business days**.
- We will keep you informed as we investigate and work on a fix.
- With your permission, we will credit you once an issue is resolved.

## Scope

In scope: the Outage Observer Worker and its endpoints (`outage.observer`), the
Slack/Discord/Telegram bots, and this repository.

Out of scope: the upstream providers we monitor; our hosting provider
(Cloudflare — report to them directly); denial-of-service testing; and social
engineering.

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue or support legal action against researchers who act in good
faith, avoid privacy violations and service disruption, and give us a reasonable
chance to remediate before any public disclosure.
